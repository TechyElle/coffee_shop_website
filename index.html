Welcome to Mood Cafe & Lounge Bar

Nestled along Bonifacio Street in Infanta, Quezon, Mood Cafe and Lounge Bar is where your day begins with a perfectly brewed cup of coffee and ends with hand-crafted cocktails in a warm, intimate setting. We are more than just a place to eat and drink — we are a space to unwind, connect, and simply be.

Whether you are catching up with friends over an afternoon latte, settling in for a solo work session, or winding down the evening with a signature cocktail and live music, Mood has a seat with your name on it.

Our cozy interior — warm lights, great vibes, always welcoming.

Join the Mood Team

We are a small but passionate team dedicated to creating memorable experiences for every guest who walks through our doors. If you love people, thrive in a creative environment, and want to be part of something special in Infanta, we would love to hear from you.

📌 Current Openings

  • Barista / Cafe Staff — Part-time & Full-time
    Experience with espresso machines preferred; training provided for the right candidate.
  • Bartender / Mixologist — Full-time (evenings & weekends)
    Knowledge of classic and craft cocktails required. Creative flair is a big plus.
  • Waiter / Service Crew — Part-time & Full-time
    Friendly, reliable, and passionate about hospitality. No experience required — we train.
  • Social Media & Content Creator — Flexible / Project-based
    Strong photography or video editing skills. Knowledge of Instagram and Facebook marketing.
  • Events Coordinator — Part-time
    Help us plan and execute live music nights, private events, and special promotions.

💌 How to Apply

Send your resume and a short introduction about yourself to careers@moodcafe.ph. Use the job title as your email subject. Walk-in applications are also welcome during cafe hours — just ask for the manager and bring a printed copy of your resume.

We review all applications on a rolling basis. Shortlisted applicants will be contacted within two weeks for an informal interview over coffee — on us!
